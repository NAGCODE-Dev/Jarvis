# Training Knowledge Base

## Executive Summary

This knowledge base covers exercise science, strength and conditioning, hypertrophy, recovery, nutrition, CrossFit, weightlifting, powerlifting, youth development, and elite performance. It is organized for local RAG usage: broad first-pass files, explicit evidence notes, and high-signal source links.

## Source Strategy

- Meta-analyses and systematic reviews first.
- Official ACSM and NSCA guidance second.
- Landmark randomized and longitudinal studies third.
- Expert synthesis only when it is traceable back to primary literature.
