# Key Meta-Analyses, Systematic Reviews, and Evidence Hierarchy

## Executive Summary

Use this file as the evidence entry point. The best-supported topics in this corpus are hypertrophy volume, load, frequency, failure proximity, youth resistance training safety, and autoregulation tools.

## Evidence Interpretation Rules

- Meta-analysis > systematic review > randomized study > cohort > expert opinion.
- A meta-analysis can still be weak if included studies are heterogeneous, underpowered, or poorly controlled.
- For coaching decisions, external validity matters as much as p-values.

## Known Gaps

- Long-term elite sport intervention data remain limited.
- Many hypertrophy findings come from young adult males and may not generalize perfectly.
- CrossFit-specific controlled literature is still thinner than general resistance training literature.
