## Design Principles

- `knowledge/` stores facts, literature, official documentation, and stable conceptual maps.
- `wisdom/` stores decision rules, heuristics, and action-oriented guidance.
- Each domain is optimized for local RAG with grouped files, explicit references, and navigable hierarchy.
